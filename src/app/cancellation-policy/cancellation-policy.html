Cancellation & Refund Policy

This Cancellation & Refund Policy outlines the terms under which users may cancel bookings, request refunds, or modify scheduled services on PRx.care. We aim to provide flexibility while ensuring fairness to both patients and healthcare providers.

1 User-Initiated Cancellation

Customers may cancel their booking or service at any time before the scheduled start time through their account dashboard or by contacting support.

2 Cancellation Timeframes

Time of Cancellation Policy
24 hours or more before scheduled time Full cancellation allowed with no charges
Less than 24 hours before scheduled time Cancellation fee may apply
After service start / No-show No cancellation or refund allowed

3 How to Cancel

  • Log in to your account and navigate to your bookings.
  • Select the booking and click on the cancel option.
  • Alternatively, contact customer support for assistance.

4 Service Provider Cancellation

In rare cases where a service must be cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances, users will be notified promptly and offered either a full refund or the option to reschedule.

5 Modification Policy

Changes to bookings (rescheduling) may be allowed depending on provider availability and must be made within the allowed cancellation timeframe.

6 Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Full refunds are provided for cancellations within the eligible timeframe.
  • Partial refunds may be issued for late cancellations.
  • No refunds are provided for completed services or no-shows.

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days. The actual time may vary depending on your payment provider.

Mode of Refund

All refunds will be credited to the original payment method used at the time of booking.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Missed appointments or no-shows
  • Last-minute cancellations beyond the allowed timeframe
  • Promotional or discounted bookings (unless otherwise specified)

Refund Issues: If you experience any issues with your refund, please contact our support team with your booking details.
