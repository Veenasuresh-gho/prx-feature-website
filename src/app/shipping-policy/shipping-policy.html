1 Delivery Coverage

  • Delivery is available in select cities and pin codes across India. Availability is displayed at checkout based on your delivery address.
  • Coverage depends on which pharmacy partners operate in your area.
  • Certain specialised medicines may have more limited coverage due to storage and handling requirements.

3 Order Processing

  • Order Confirmation: Your order is confirmed only after successful payment authorisation.
  • Prescription Verification: Orders containing Schedule H, H1, or X medicines require a valid digital prescription. Orders that fail verification will be cancelled and fully refunded.
  • Substitution Policy: If an ordered medicine is out of stock, you must approve any suggested generic substitute before dispatch.
  • Order Modification: Orders can be modified or cancelled before the pharmacy confirms dispatch. Once dispatched, modifications are not possible.

5 Order Tracking

  • Track your order live in the PRx app under Bookings > Pharmacy Orders
  • Status updates are sent via SMS and push notification at each stage
  • A live GPS tracking link may be shared via SMS upon dispatch
  • If your order shows "Delivered" but was not received, contact us at support@prx.care or call +91 88484 41220

6 Returns & Refunds

Non-Returnable Items

  • Prescription medicines and controlled substances
  • Temperature-sensitive or cold-chain products
  • Opened or partially used products
  • Products with broken seals or tampered packaging
  • Personal hygiene and single-use medical devices

Eligible Refund / Replacement Scenarios

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Expired product delivered
  • Damaged or broken product received (photographic evidence required within 48 hours)
  • Order cancelled before dispatch
  • Prescription verification failure resulting in order cancellation
  • Non-delivery (product not received despite "Delivered" status)

7 Failed Deliveries

An order may be marked as failed delivery if the address is incorrect, you are unavailable after two delivery attempts, or access to the delivery location is denied. Our delivery partner will make one additional attempt within the same day before returning the order to the pharmacy.

8 Prescription Medicines

Prescription medicines require a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner. PRx.care complies fully with the Drugs and Cosmetics Act 1940 and all applicable regulations.

  • A digital prescription uploaded via the PRx app is accepted. Physical copies may be required for Schedule X drugs.
  • Our licensed pharmacists review all prescriptions before dispensing. Forged or invalid prescriptions will result in order cancellation and may be reported to authorities.
  • PRx.care operates a Medicine Subscription service for chronic condition patients. Subscriptions are auto-renewed monthly; cancellation can be managed via the app.

9 Responsibility

  • PRx.care's role: We act as a technology facilitator connecting users with partner pharmacies. We do not manufacture, store, or physically handle medicines.
  • Product quality: The quality, authenticity, and proper storage of medicines are the responsibility of the dispensing pharmacy partner.
  • Your responsibility: Ensure the delivery address is accurate; have a responsible adult present to receive prescription medicines.
  • Force Majeure: PRx.care is not liable for delivery failures from events beyond reasonable control.
