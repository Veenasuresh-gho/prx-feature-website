1 Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide you with safe, effective, and personalised healthcare services.

Personal Identification Information

  • Full name, date of birth, and gender
  • Email address and mobile phone number
  • Address, pin code, and geographic location (when you grant permission)
  • Government-issued IDs such as Aadhaar number or ABHA Health ID
  • Profile photograph (optional)

Health & Medical Information

  • Medical history, diagnoses, and ongoing conditions
  • Prescriptions (e-prescriptions) and medication lists
  • Lab and diagnostic reports uploaded or generated via PRx
  • Appointment records, consultation notes, and doctor feedback
  • Vital signs and health metrics (when entered via the app)
  • Insurance information and health plan details

Financial Information

  • Payment method details (processed by PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways)
  • Transaction history for appointments, pharmacy orders, and home care bookings

Technical & Usage Information

  • IP address, device type, operating system, and browser version
  • App usage patterns, feature interactions, and session duration
  • Crash logs and error reports to improve platform stability
  • Cookies, local storage, and analytics identifiers (see Section 8)

Provider Information

  • Facility name, type, registration number, and licensing information
  • Contact person details and designated PRx administrator credentials
  • Bank account / settlement details for payment disbursement

2 How We Use Your Data

Every piece of data we collect serves a defined, legitimate purpose.

  • Deliver core healthcare services: facilitate doctor consultations, lab test bookings, pharmacy orders, and home care services.
  • Personalise your experience: display relevant providers, recommend specialists, and surface health insights.
  • Process transactions securely: handle payments, generate receipts, manage refunds, and maintain financial history.
  • Send service communications: appointment confirmations, reminders, prescription notifications, and delivery updates.
  • Support ABDM integration: link your ABHA ID to enable interoperable health record sharing.
  • Improve and secure our platform: analyse aggregate usage to fix bugs, improve UX, and detect fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations: fulfil requirements under the IT Act 2000, DPDP Act 2023, and ABDM guidelines.
  • Provide customer support: respond to your queries, complaints, and feedback.

No Marketing Without Consent: We will never send you unsolicited promotional communications. You may opt out at any time from account settings.

3 Data Sharing

Your data is shared only on a strict need-to-know basis to facilitate the services you have requested.

Healthcare Providers Within the PRx Network

Verified doctors, clinics, labs, and pharmacies receive only the information required to deliver the specific service you have booked. All providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Payment Service Providers

PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways receive only the payment data required to complete your transaction and are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

ABDM / NHA (National Health Authority)

Where you have explicitly linked your ABHA ID and consented to health record sharing, certain data may be exchanged within the ABDM ecosystem.

Legal & Regulatory Authorities

We may disclose information to government agencies or law enforcement bodies when required by law or a valid court order.

🚫 We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal or health data to any third party for commercial or marketing purposes — ever.

4 Data Security

  • AES-256 encryption for all health data at rest; TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
  • Role-based access controls (RBAC) ensuring staff and providers access only relevant data.
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all provider and administrator accounts.
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits by independent third-party experts.
  • ISO 27001-aligned information security management practices.
  • Incident response plan — affected users notified within 72 hours of a data breach.

⚠️ Important: No digital system can guarantee absolute security. Report any suspicious activity to support@prx.care immediately.

5 Your Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Right to Restriction: Ask us to limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Revoke any previously given consent at any time.
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing, including profiling.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer.

Contact our DPO at admin@gho.care or support@prx.care. We will respond within 30 days.
